Camp Marymount

Camp Marymount has been around for nearly 70 years, offering a summer camp experience on 340 acres of beautiful middle Tennessee land and water. Camp holds a special place in the hearts of the Menke family. Becca worked at camp for a summer, and many of Becca's friends are repeat campers or staff. Becca and Mikey are extremely excited to have everybody share their love in a place they love so much.

22 cabins will be available for campers wedding guests for a fun community vibe! Each cabin has electricity, running water, heating, air conditioning, a roof and accomodates approximately 8 people. Don't forget to bring your sleeping bag and a towel!
